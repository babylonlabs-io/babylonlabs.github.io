# BABY Staking via Mintscan

## 1. Access Mintscan

Open the Mintscan website: [Mintscan](https://www.mintscan.io).

## 2. Navigate to the Staking Page

1. On the homepage, click on **“Stake.”** This will take you to the staking page.

## 3. Connect Your Wallet

1. The staking page will prompt you to **connect your wallet**. Click on the option to connect your wallet.

<ThemedImage
  alt="HoodScan Baby Faucet"
  sources={{

    dark: useBaseUrl('/img/guides/baby_stakers/via_mintscan/2_dark.png'),
  }}
/>
2. Select the digital wallet you are using (such as **Keplr**).
3. Follow the prompts to complete the connection.

  alt="HoodScan Baby Faucet"
  sources={{

## 4. Select the Token

1. After connecting your wallet, you will need to select the token for staking.
