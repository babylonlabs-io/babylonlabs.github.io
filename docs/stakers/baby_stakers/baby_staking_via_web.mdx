# BABY Staking via Web

A step by step guide to staking BABY tokens on the Babylon Genesis mainnet using the web.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- a crypto wallet compatible with the BABY Staking Website installed on your browser
- a Babylon Genesis mainnet wallet created
- acquired BABY tokens

## Step by Step Guide

### 1. Access the BABY Staking Website

Open the BABY Staking website:

https://staking.babylonlabs.io/baby

### 4. Choose a Validator

4.1 Click the plus sign on **Select Validator**

<ThemedImage
  alt="BABY Staking via Web Step 4.1"
  sources={{
    light: useBaseUrl('/img/guides/baby_stakers/via_web/click_plus.png'),
    dark: useBaseUrl('/img/guides/baby_stakers/via_web/click_plus_dark.png'),

4.2 Validators verify transactions, propose and confirm new blocks, and help maintain the security and consensus of Babylon Genesis. Here, you can view the Validator name, Voting Power, Commission, and Total Staked. You can also use the dropdown to filter Active, Inactive, Jailed, and Slashed Validators. Select your chosen Validator.

### 5. Preview Your Transaction

You will see an overview of your transaction, including Validator, Stake Amount, and Transaction Fees.
<ThemedImage
  alt="BABY Staking via Web Step 5"
  sources={{
    light: useBaseUrl('/img/guides/baby_stakers/via_web/preview.png'),
    dark: useBaseUrl('/img/guides/baby_stakers/via_web/preview_dark.png'),

/>

Please note: The staking transaction may take up to about five hours to process. Funds will not be deducted instantly and a sufficient available balance must be maintained until the transaction is confirmed and the deduction is finalized.

### 6. Stake

6.1 Click **Stake**<br/>
6.2 Approve your transaction in your web wallet

  }}
/>

6.3 Your BABY staking request has been submitted. Stakes activate within about one hour. Until activation is complete, keep the staked amount in your wallet to ensure successful processing.
