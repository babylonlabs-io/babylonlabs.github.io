# Staking Guides

Babylon Genesis supports BTC staking and BABY staking. BTC staking lets Bitcoin holders
participate from the Bitcoin chain without wrapping or bridging BTC. BABY staking lets
BABY holders delegate to validators on Babylon Genesis.

Stakers who participate in both BTC staking and BABY staking can earn additional rewards
through [co-staking](/stakers/co_staking/).
